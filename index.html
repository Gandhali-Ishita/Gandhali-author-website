About the Author

Gandhali Ishita

Gandhali Ishita writes emotionally charged romance and crime-driven stories with dangerous love, strong women, spiritual undertones, and characters fighting to survive the worlds that shaped them.

Her storytelling blends passion, pain, loyalty, betrayal, and the kind of love that does not stay quiet — the kind that changes everyone it touches.
