About the Author

Gandhali Ishita

Gandhali Ishita writes emotionally charged romance and crime-driven stories with strong women, dangerous love, spiritual undertones, and characters who fight to survive what life throws at them.

Her stories blend passion, pain, loyalty, secrets, betrayal, and the kind of love that refuses to stay quiet.
